Social Hub tutorial

Instagram Requirments

  • To activate your instagram inside the Social Hub, we need the following items:
    • Client ID
    • Client Secret
    • Application ID
    • "Slug Name"



    Steps

    1. Creating an application in instagram

    To create an instagram APP you need to go to: https://www.instagram.com/developer

    FYI: If your user is already an instagram developer, please skip this step.

     

    To create a instagram APP you first need to become a instagram developer by registering and acepting their Terms & Privacy.

    2) Creating an APP

    Click on "Register New Client" to create a new aplication

    Define the Application name as "GotChosen APP", The discription as "This app is to comuicate with gotchosen Social Hub", The company name as "GotChosen", The Website URL, Vaild redirect URL, and the Privacy Policy URL as "https://www.gotchosen.com" and lastly click on "Register"

     

     

    3) Obtaining the "Client ID" and "Client Secret"

    Inside the application settings, find the "GotChosen APP" we just created above and click on "Manage"

    The "Client ID" and "Client Secret" should be shown, and that is exactly what we need.
