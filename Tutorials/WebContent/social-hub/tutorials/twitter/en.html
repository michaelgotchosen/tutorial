Twitter Requirments

To Activate your twitter inside the Social Hub, We need the following:

  • Consumer Key (API Key);
  • Consumer Secret (API Secret)
  • Your Twitter's "slug name"

Steps

To create a Twitter application you will need to go to: https://dev.twitter.com

  1. Creating a Twitter App

To create a twitter application click on "My apps" on the top menu inside the Developer dashbaord.

After Click on "Create New App":

Define the Twitter's App name as GotChosen APP, and also define the description as: Publisher Application - GotChosen

Inside the Website field, write https://gotchosen.com, dont forget to accept "Twitter Developer Agreement" and lastly just click on "Create your Twitter application".

  1. Obtaining your "Consumer Key" and "Consumer Secret".

After creating your App, as we just did above, you will be taken to this Modal below. There you will navigate to "Keys and Access Tokens" tab.

Your Consumer key (API Key), and Consumer Secert (API Secert) will be shown below
